Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to TechVantage ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our services, or interact with our AI-powered solutions.

Our Commitment: TechVantage operates under the principle of data minimization and transparency. We only collect information necessary to provide our services and improve your experience.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following types of personal information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, business address
  • Business Information: Company name, business type, industry, website URL
  • Project Details: Service requirements, project specifications, budget information
  • Communication Records: Chat logs, email correspondence, support tickets
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely through third-party providers)

2.2 Technical Information

  • Website Analytics: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session data, preferences, user behavior patterns
  • AI Interaction Data: Conversations with our AI chatbots, usage patterns of AI tools
  • Performance Data: System logs, error reports, service usage statistics

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information from:

  • Social media platforms (when you connect or interact with us)
  • Business partners and referral sources
  • Public databases and business directories
  • Marketing and advertising platforms

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Delivery

  • Providing website development, app creation, and AI solutions
  • Customizing AI chatbots and automation systems for your business
  • Managing project timelines and deliverables
  • Providing customer support and technical assistance

3.2 Business Operations

  • Processing payments and managing invoices
  • Communicating about projects and services
  • Improving our services and developing new solutions
  • Conducting market research and business analysis

3.3 Marketing and Communication

  • Sending service updates and newsletters (with your consent)
  • Sharing relevant industry insights and AI trends
  • Promoting new services and special offers
  • Conducting customer satisfaction surveys

4. AI and Machine Learning

AI Data Usage: As an AI solutions provider, we may use anonymized and aggregated data to improve our AI models and services. This helps us create better solutions for all our clients.

4.1 AI Training and Improvement

  • Using anonymized conversation data to improve chatbot responses
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance AI recommendation engines
  • Developing better automation workflows based on client feedback
  • Creating industry-specific AI templates and solutions

4.2 Data Protection in AI Systems

  • All personal data used in AI training is anonymized and encrypted
  • We implement differential privacy techniques where applicable
  • Client-specific AI models are kept separate and confidential
  • Regular audits ensure AI systems comply with privacy standards

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do Not Sell Your Information

TechVantage does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

5.2 Authorized Sharing

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who help us deliver services (hosting, payment processing, analytics)
  • Business Partners: Trusted partners involved in your project delivery (with your consent)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or regulatory authority
  • Business Transfers: In case of merger, acquisition, or sale of business assets
  • Protection of Rights: To protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of others

6. Data Security

6.1 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data transmission and storage uses industry-standard encryption
  • Access Controls: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • Regular Audits: Quarterly security assessments and penetration testing
  • Secure Infrastructure: Cloud hosting with enterprise-grade security features
  • Employee Training: Regular security awareness training for all staff

6.2 Data Breach Response

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Report to relevant authorities as required by law
  • Provide detailed information about the breach and our response
  • Offer assistance and support to affected individuals

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

7.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our website
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements (with consent)

7.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may affect website functionality.

8. Your Rights and Choices

8.1 Data Subject Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Rectification: Correct any inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal requirements)
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction: Limit how we process your information
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for processing based on consent

8.2 Marketing Communications

You can opt out of marketing communications at any time by:

  • Clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails
  • Contacting us directly at privacy@techvantage.in
  • Updating your preferences in your account settings

9. Data Retention

9.1 Retention Periods

  • Project Data: Retained for 3 years after project completion
  • Communication Records: Kept for 2 years for support purposes
  • Marketing Data: Retained until you opt out or for 5 years, whichever is sooner
  • Legal/Compliance Data: Retained as required by applicable laws

9.2 Secure Deletion

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete it using industry-standard methods to prevent recovery.

10. International Data Transfers

TechVantage is based in Jaipur, India. If you are located outside India, please note that your information may be transferred to and processed in India. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you of significant changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending email notifications for material changes
  • Requiring renewed consent where necessary
