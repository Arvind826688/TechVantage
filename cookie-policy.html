Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and tracking technologies on our website

Last Updated: December 2024

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit websites. They help websites remember information about your visit, such as your preferences and actions, making your browsing experience more efficient and personalized.

Quick Definition: Think of cookies as digital sticky notes that websites place on your device to remember useful information about your preferences and behavior.

2. How TechVantage Uses Cookies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our website, analyze usage patterns, and improve our AI-powered services. This policy explains what cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can control them.

2.1 Our Cookie Philosophy

  • Transparency: We clearly explain what cookies we use and why
  • Control: You have the choice to accept or decline non-essential cookies
  • Minimal Data: We only collect what's necessary for functionality and improvement
  • Security: All cookie data is processed securely and protected

8. How to Manage Cookies

8.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

Browser-Specific Instructions:

Google Chrome

Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies and other site data

Mozilla Firefox

Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data

Safari

Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data

Microsoft Edge

Settings → Site permissions → Cookies and site data

8.2 Our Cookie Settings

You can also manage your cookie preferences directly on our website:

  • Click the "Cookie Settings" link in our website footer
  • Use the cookie banner that appears on your first visit
  • Contact us at privacy@techvantage.in for assistance
  • Clear your browser cookies to reset all preferences

8.3 Impact of Disabling Cookies

Disabling certain types of cookies may affect your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Website may not function properly
  • Functional Cookies: Loss of personalized settings and preferences
  • Analytics Cookies: We can't improve our services based on usage data
  • Marketing Cookies: Less relevant advertisements and offers

9. Mobile App Tracking

9.1 Mobile Tracking Technologies

If you use our mobile applications, we may use similar tracking technologies:

  • Device Identifiers: IDFA (iOS) and GAID (Android) for analytics
  • App Usage Analytics: Feature usage and crash reporting
  • Push Notification Tokens: For relevant service updates
  • Local Storage: App preferences and cached data

9.2 Mobile Privacy Controls

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Reset advertising ID
  • App Settings: Privacy controls within our mobile apps

10. Data Retention and Deletion

10.1 Cookie Data Retention

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Stored for the duration specified in cookie settings
  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 26 months (Google Analytics standard)
  • Marketing Data: Deleted after 13 months or when you opt out

10.2 How to Delete Cookies

  1. Clear Browser Data: Delete all cookies from your browser settings
  2. Selective Deletion: Remove specific cookies from individual websites
  3. Automatic Deletion: Set browser to delete cookies on exit
  4. Contact Us: Request deletion of server-side cookie data

11. International Data Transfers

Some of our third-party cookie providers may transfer your data internationally. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses with international providers
  • Adequacy decisions for data transfers to approved countries
  • Privacy Shield framework compliance (where applicable)
  • Regular review of international transfer mechanisms

12. Children's Privacy
